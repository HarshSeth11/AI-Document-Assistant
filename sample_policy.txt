LEAVE POLICY
Employees are entitled to 20 days of paid annual leave per year. Leave must be applied for at least 2 weeks in advance. Unused leave can be carried forward to the next year up to a maximum of 10 days.

WORK FROM HOME POLICY
Employees may work from home up to 3 days per week with manager approval. A stable internet connection is required.

TRAVEL POLICY
Business travel must be approved by department head. Economy class standard for flights under 6 hours.
