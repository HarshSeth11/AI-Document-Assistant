CODE OF CONDUCT
All employees are expected to maintain professional behavior. Harassment and discrimination are strictly prohibited. Violations must be reported to HR within 24 hours.

NDA AND CONFIDENTIALITY
Employees must sign an NDA before accessing sensitive systems. Sharing confidential information externally is grounds for termination.

401K RETIREMENT PLAN
The company offers a 401k plan with up to 4% employer matching. Employees are eligible after 6 months.

PERFORMANCE REVIEWS
Reviews are conducted twice yearly in June and December. A rating of 3 or above is required for annual increment.
